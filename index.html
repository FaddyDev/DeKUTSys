Exam Processing System

First Client: DeKUT

DeKUT Digital Exam Processing System - DeDEPS

DeDEPS is a digital exam system suited for a university environment but can be customized to suit other needs. The operations range from adding of students and lecturers, assigning lecturers to units, adding units to semesters, adding students to semesters (promotion), adding marks, and generating exam reports, among others. The system is web-based so lecturers can enter marks remotely. The system has a general admin user for overall operations and administration, a school admin for adding school-level units and generating transcripts, a chairperson of department for assigning units to lecturers, a lecturer user for entering exam marks, and an exam coordinator for generating reports such as scoresheets, passlists, consolidated marksheets, graduation lists, and transcripts. Students can access ready transcripts from their portal. Click here to look at the application. Contact us for more info.

Recruitment Portal

First Client: DeKUT

Recruitment Portal

The Recruitment Portal is a web based system for recruitment and selection. Job seekers register, providing their information then apply for jobs when vacancies are declared. The HR registry then reviews the applications and selects successful applicants. The system helps in the selection process by comparing applicant details and the selection criteria. Admissions System

The Admissions System is used for admitting students into the university but can be modified to suit client needs. The system allows potential students to view courses and then make their applications online. The institution then reviews applications and responds accordingly.
